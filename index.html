FAQs

What are the apartment options available and their sizes?

We currently offer apartments in 4 different models ranging from 142m2 to 165m2.

Do the apartments have a garden?

There are two apartment options available with a garden in the ground floor. Ground A has a garden of 23m2 and Ground B as a garden of 102m2.

Are the apartments furnished?

We currently don't offer furnished apartments.

What payment plans do you offer?

For your convenience, you can pay in equal installments for up to 36 months. 36% of the total payment is to be paid as a downpayment.

Do you offer any cash discounts?

Yes, we do offer special discounts for cash payments.

Where is the apartment building located?

The proposed apartments are located on North 90 Road and Suez Road
